What is NSBE?

The National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE) is one of the largest student-governed organizations based in the United States. NSBE, founded in 1975, supports and promotes the aspirations of collegiate and pre-collegiate students and technical professionals in engineering and technology.

About us

We are a proud region IV chapter, which works to support our members while also utilizing our resources and networking opportunities to learn about engineering. We host general meetings where expert engineers are invited to speak to our members about internships and job prospects. Professional development courses (resume writing, elevator pitches, interviewing skills, and so on) are also held at these sessions to assist our members in finding job prospects in their industry.
